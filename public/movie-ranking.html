My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Matrix

Unique movie in it's day. A take on the writers
idea of life and how we look at the way we see
the world.

Independance Day

Another movie with advanced graphics. Aliens invade
Earth and humans prevail.

Avengers Endgame

A comic turned movie. Thanos has wiped out half
of life across the universe but The Avengers save
the day!
